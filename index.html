About Us

Make My Class was inspired by the founder’s love of knowledge and appreciation for teaching our youth. With Make My Class, teachers and students alike are able to utilize a space for classes, whether its one-time or recurring.

The facility provides fully furnished classrooms that are an optimal space for teaching, learning, training, and more. Sessions that take place here are clean, comfortable, and quiet, which makes it an excellent environment for learning.

The Make My Class staff works with each of their customers on scheduling and classroom booking.

Who Are We?

The ‘Make My Class’ ’ is a business title to the rent business of furnished classrooms. The office of make my class manages the time schedules and booking of classes. Make my class : It simply stands as asking for making a class. The ‘Make My Class’ management provides managing classrooms to teachers and students who is willing to make his/her class in the classrooms provided by the management for the same.
